## SECTION 6: SUPPLIER STRATEGY

### Supplier Sourcing Hierarchy

**Level 1 — Local Gorakhpur (Start here)**
- Advantage: Zero transport, inspect before buying, no minimum order
- Disadvantage: Price may be 5–12% higher than source state
- Use for: First 3 months until cash flow stabilises

**Level 2 — Lucknow/Kanpur (Month 3+)**
- Advantage: 8–15% cheaper on spices sourced from Rajasthan/MP
- Transport: ₹2–4/kg on 100kg+ shipments = net saving after transport
- Use for: Orders above ₹15,000 per product

**Level 3 — Direct from source state (Year 1, Q4+)**
- Nizamabad (Telangana) for turmeric: Direct farmer/processor contact
- Rajasthan for coriander/cumin: Jodhpur/Kota wholesale
- Guntur (Andhra Pradesh) for chilli: Byadagi/Guntur processors
- Only viable when order size exceeds 500kg per commodity

### Supplier Qualification Checklist

Before buying from any supplier:
- [ ] Ask for sample (minimum 100g) — test colour, aroma, moisture
- [ ] Check if FSSAI certified
- [ ] Ask for batch date and source origin
- [ ] Negotiate payment: 50% advance, 50% on delivery (or full cash for first deal)
- [ ] Take WhatsApp number — build relationship for repeat orders
- [ ] Compare with at least 2 other suppliers before first large order

### Building Supplier Relationships

- Always pay on time (or early) — this unlocks priority supply and credit later
- Target: By Month 6, get 30-day credit from 2 regular suppliers
- Family pharmacy wholesale network = social proof → use family's reputation to get better terms

### Ultra-Low Capital Inventory Model (₹50K Phase)

- **Rule 1:** Never hold more than 25 days of inventory
- **Rule 2:** Reorder when stock falls below 10 days' supply
- **Rule 3:** Never pay for goods you cannot sell in 30 days
- **Rule 4:** Sell 70%+ of each batch before ordering next batch
- **Rule 5:** Keep ₹15,000+ in cash reserve at all times

### Warehouse Strategy (Phase 1)

- **Use family flour mill premises** — saves ₹5,000–10,000/month on warehouse rent
- Dedicate one dry, cool, ventilated corner (minimum 8×10 feet)
- Pallets/wooden platforms: Elevate stock off floor (moisture protection)
- Rat/pest prevention: Boric acid traps; sealed bags only
- Thermometer: Keep area below 28°C; above 32°C risks colour fade in chilli

### FIFO Protocol

- All incoming stock goes behind existing stock on shelf
- Date each batch with marker pen on bag
- Older stock always dispatched first

### Expiry Management

- Red Chilli powder: Mark "Best Before 9 months from pack date" — sell within 6 months
- Turmeric: Mark "Best Before 12 months from pack date"
- Conduct monthly audit: Any stock older than 80% of shelf life → offer to retailers at 10% discount

## SECTION 8: PACKAGING PLAN

### Phase 1 Packaging Strategy (₹50K Model)

With ₹50K capital, offset-printed pouches (MOQ 50,000+) are not viable. Use:

**Option A — Universal + Sticker (Recommended for Launch)**
- Buy silver/metalized BOPP flat pouches (no print, transparent window)
- Apply digital-printed PP sticker labels
- Cost: ₹1.80–2.20 (pouch) + ₹0.60 (sticker) = ₹2.40–2.80/pack
- MOQ: 500 units from Betiahata market or Kanpur packaging vendors
- Advantage: Flexible, can change design without reprinting pouches
- Disadvantage: Less premium look than pre-printed pouches

**Option B — Pre-printed Digital Flexible Pouches (Month 3+)**
- Digital-printed flexible pouches (Stand-up / Flat seal)
- MOQ: 2,000–3,000 units per SKU
- Cost: ₹3.50–5.00/pouch (100g), ₹5.00–7.50/pouch (500g)
- Advantage: Premium look, builds brand identity faster
- Source: Delhi packaging vendors (Daryaganj/Okhla Industrial Area), Kanpur flexible packaging

**Retailer Margin:** 18–22% (ensure retailer makes ₹3–5/pack profit)
**Distributor Margin (Year 1 — avoid distributors, self-distribute):** 10–12%

### Pricing Principles

1. **Never compete on MRP with nationals.** Position 10–15% below MDH/Everest, 20–30% above loose/unbranded.
2. **Cash discounts:** 2% extra discount for cash payment on same day. Incentivises fast payment.
3. **Volume slab:** Buy 10 packs of same SKU → 1 extra pack free (effective 9% volume discount; maintain margin).
4. **No credit for first 6 months.** Exceptions only for stores with 3+ months purchase history.
5. **MRP must be printed on pack.** Non-negotiable for FSSAI compliance and brand integrity.

### Psychological Pricing Rules

- 100g at ₹24 feels "affordable trial" vs ₹25 (round number feels more expensive)
- 500g at ₹99 beats ₹100 (sub-100 barrier)
- Never price below unbranded loose — signals quality doubt
